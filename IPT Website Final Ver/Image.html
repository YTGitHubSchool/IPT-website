Higher quality images have become a lot easier to obtain or create now, anyone with access to the internet can easily download images with high resolution. High quality images significantly impact the performance of a multimedia product and take up lots of storage. The storage requirements of images are dependent on the number, size, tone and colour of each pixel. The bit depth of an image determines the number of colours that an image can have.
